Funny women and non-binary people in the UK and Ireland

Our panel show stats show that women have been under-represented on comedy panel shows. Some panel shows have claimed it is hard to find funny women. If you are looking for some funny women or non-binary folks in the UK and Ireland here is a non-exhaustive list of suggestions. Thanks to Pippa Evans, What The Frock?, Paco B. Garcia, and The Guilty Feminist/Made of Human podcasts for lots of suggestions. Feel free to tell me more.
