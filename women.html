Funny women in the UK and Ireland

If you are looking for some funny women in the UK and Ireland here is a non-exhaustive list of suggestions. Thanks to Pippa Evans, What The Frock?, Paco B. Garcia, and The Guilty Feminist/Made of Human podcasts for lots of suggestions. Feel free to tweet me with more.
